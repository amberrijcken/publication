AMBER RIJCKEN

My work has found its roots in a believe that, in this society, the most radical and effective protest that can overturn the corporate powers, is growing your own food. The process of working directly in harmony with nature, is in my opinion the most essential thing that can be done to change people form inside.

WORK

In this project I try to shape my thoughts and dreams of living completely self-sufficient and in harmony with nature. I try to collect all kind of information: about ecological construction, permaculture, old techniques and all the animals and plants that fit in the eco-system I want to work with. In this way I'm building my own archive. The information I collect in this archive I hope to actually use in my realization of a farm.

It is my dream to make this 'permaculture farm' real, and because of that I started the project "painting for a farm". It try to paint all the animals, plants, trees etc.etc that fit in the eco-system I want to work whit on little white wooden panels. I also paint all kind of diffrent landscapes and farmes, that I call 'wish-gardens'. 

All my work I'll try to sell, and all the money I'll raise will go directly to the realization of the farm. But this will be something for the future.

PAINTINGS

I strongly believe in the power of imagination. Whit sketches that I've made in the park or in the forest, photograps and memories, I try to make "wish-gardens". Those gardens are a way for me to shape my thought and dreams of living completely self-sufficient and in harmony with nature.

The sketches I that I made, I use for my paintings.
